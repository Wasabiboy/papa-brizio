Our Story

A taste of Italy
by the bay

Nestled in the heart of Mairangi Bay, Papà Brizio is where flavor, comfort, and style meet. Whether you're after a relaxed morning coffee, a cozy brunch, or evening cocktails with friends, Papa's offers something special for every moment of the day.

Start your mid-morning with artisan coffee and fresh pastries, or savor our chef-crafted brunch dishes made with locally sourced ingredients. By afternoon, enjoy light bites and vibrant bistro favorites.

As the sun sets, we transform into a lively bar with curated wines, craft beers, homemade Limoncello and signature cocktails — best enjoyed with our delicious pizza, pasta and risotto. Don't miss the slow-cooked lamb shanks.

Come for the coffee. Stay for the atmosphere.
